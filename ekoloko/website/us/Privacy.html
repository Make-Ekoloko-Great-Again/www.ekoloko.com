1.3 Cookies

We use “cookies” to save User name, password and language selection for future log-ins to the Site, to better understand how Users interact with the Site, and to monitor web traffic routing on the Site and aggregate usage of the Site.

Cookies are small text files that a website can use to recognize repeat Users, to facilitate the User's ongoing access to and use of the website, and to compile aggregate data to improve the website. Cookies work as follows: We will send a cookie to a User’s browser that uniquely associates that User’s computer with information stored at the Site. That User’s browser places the cookie in its cookies file. When that User next accesses the Site on his or her computer, the Site recognizes the cookie, makes available the stored information (such as User names, password and language selection), and may add new information based on the present visit.
