About ekoloko

ekoloko is a virtual community where kids discover the world, virtual and real, through fun games and quests, while absorbing positive values, knowledge and skills that will help them become more responsible and involved individuals.

At ekoloko we have created the environment that we wanted our own kids to grow in. An environment which is safe, supportive and encourages positive behavior and values. ekoloko offers kids a wide range of their favorite activities like playing and hanging out with their friends. Our storyline and the journey the kids go through as part of the ekolokian community create an environment where taking responsibility, friendship, leadership, acceptance and awareness are not only big words but also values one can live by.
